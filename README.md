# 5 Little Monkeys Jumping on the Bed

A toddler-friendly game based on the classic nursery rhyme.

## How to Play

Press **D F G H J** to make the monkeys jump!

After a few jumps, they'll fall off and bonk their heads. Press **SPACE** to call the doctor and tuck them into bed.

Game ends when all 5 monkeys are sleeping tight.

## Play

Just open `index.html` in any browser.
